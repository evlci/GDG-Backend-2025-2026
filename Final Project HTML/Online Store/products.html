Product One

A refrigerator, also known as a fridge, is a household appliance used to keep food and drinks cold and fresh. It works by removing heat from the inside and maintaining a low temperature. Refrigerators help prevent food spoilage and extend the shelf life of perishable items such as fruits, vegetables, dairy products, and meat.

Product Two

A washing machine is a household appliance used to clean clothes and fabrics automatically. It saves time and effort compared to washing by hand. Washing machines come in different types, such as front-loading and top-loading, and offer multiple wash cycles for various fabrics and levels of dirt.

Product Three

A freezer is a household appliance used to store food at very low temperatures, keeping it frozen for long periods. It helps preserve perishable items such as meat, vegetables, ice cream, and ready-to-eat meals. Freezers are available as standalone units or as a compartment in a refrigerator.
